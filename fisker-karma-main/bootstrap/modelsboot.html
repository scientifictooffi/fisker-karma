Model "S"

The Fisker Ocean was Fisker's venture into the electric SUV market. Unlike the Karma and Revero, the Ocean was a fully electric SUV designed to cater to a broader range of customers seeking a sustainable and spacious vehicle. It featured a modern and clean design with a spacious interior and a focus on sustainability, using recycled materials and emphasizing a more eco-friendly driving experience. The Ocean was marketed as an affordable electric SUV and aimed to compete with other electric vehicles in the growing EV market.

Model "B"

The Fisker Karma Revero was a successor to the original Karma and was designed to retain the distinctive style while improving upon its technology and performance. It featured a similar plug-in hybrid powertrain but came with enhancements to the electric range and performance. The Revero maintained the elegant and timeless design that the Karma was known for, making it a desirable option for environmentally conscious luxury car enthusiasts.

Model "D"

The Fisker Karma was the company's initial flagship model, originally introduced in 2011. It was a sleek and luxurious plug-in hybrid sports sedan that gained significant attention for its innovative design and sustainable approach. The Karma featured a hybrid powertrain, combining an electric motor with a gasoline engine that served as a range extender. The car was lauded for its stunning aesthetics, with a long, low-slung body and solar panels integrated into the roof to help charge the battery.
